About

Hello

I am a second-year student at the University of Waterloo studying Global Business and Digital Arts with a strong passion for design and creativity. Skilled in Adobe Creative Cloud, I use Illustrator, Photoshop, and Premiere Pro to bring my ideas to life, blending visual storytelling with strategy. As a certified lifeguard and competitive swim coach with 16 years of experience, I’m dedicated to inspiring emerging athletes, fostering resilience, independence, and a love for sports. I’m also passionate about marketing and advertising, where I aim to create meaningful, impactful connections through my work. My experience in animal and environmental conservation, as well as humanitarian work around the world has impacted my work and creative process. I believe in designing to contribute to a worthy cause and making a positive impact.
